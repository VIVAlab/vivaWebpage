Overview

The objective of Forward Leading Car Detection is to provide a recording and alarming subsystem for driver assistant system. It is used to detect leading cars that possibly cause a collision soon after, avoiding putting the driver into dangers. The detection system is implenmented via deep learning technique and will be embeded into Android mobile phone eventually. (The project is still in progress.)
